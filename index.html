About Us

We have created a fictional website to control the city issues. Like every growing city, Bangalore also struggles with a lot of infrastructure problems. Government ofﬁcials are unable to act on them as lot of times these problems do not get reported. The city corporation feels that empowering the citizens and engaging them to take an active part in maintaining the city will be a sustainable solution. To make this possible, the corporation has decided to develop a website named Active Society, where citizens can report their issues such as Potholes in the road, Trafﬁc light not working, Garbage collection, etc
